Savory Cracker Snack Mix

Ingredients

  • 1 cups potato sticks
  • 1 cups cheddar-flavored snack crackers
  • 1 cups sourdough pretzel nuggets
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoons sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoons dried minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • 1/2 teaspoons poppy seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°.
  2. In a large bowl, combine potato sticks, crackers and pretzels.
  3. In a small saucepan, melt butter, stir in remaining ingredients.
  4. Drizzle over cracker mixture, toss to coat.
  5. Spread in a greased 15x10x1-in. baking pan.
  6. Bake until crisp and lightly browned, 10-15 minutes, stirring every 4 minutes.
  7. Cool completely in pan on a wire rack.
  8. Store in an airtight container.
