Beetroot, Watercress and Goat's Cheese Salad

Ingredients

  • 450g can baby beetroot, drained
  • 2 tsp maple syrup
  • 2 cups watercress sprigs
  • 35g baby rocket
  • 50g goat's cheese
  • 1/4 cup hazelnuts, halved, toasted

Dressing

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tsp honey

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan-forced.
  2. Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Pat beetroot dry with paper towel.
  4. Place beetroot on prepared tray.
  5. Drizzle with maple syrup.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Toss to coat.
  8. Cover with foil.
  9. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until heated through.
  10. Make Dressing: Place all ingredients in a bowl. Whisk to combine. Season.
  11. Place warm beetroot, watercress, rocket, cheese, hazelnuts and dressing in a large bowl.
  12. Toss gently to combine. Serve.

Allergens

This recipe may contain tree nut, mustard, milk and lactose.
