Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef shank or brisket
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, sliced
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • 2 packages fresh Chinese noodles
  • 4 baby bok choy, halved
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Pickled mustard greens (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the beef into large chunks and blanch in boiling water for 5 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add garlic, ginger, star anise, cinnamon stick, and bay leaves. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the beef, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and rice wine. Stir to combine.
  4. Pour in the beef broth and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender.
  5. Season with salt and sugar to taste.
  6. Cook the Chinese noodles according to package instructions. In the last 2 minutes of cooking, add the baby bok choy to blanch.
  7. Divide the noodles and bok choy among bowls. Ladle the beef and broth over the noodles.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions and pickled mustard greens, if desired. Serve hot.

Allergens

This dish contains soy (soy sauce) and gluten (Chinese noodles).
