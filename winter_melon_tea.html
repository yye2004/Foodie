Winter Melon Tea

Ingredients

  • 500g winter melon, peeled and diced
  • 100g rock sugar
  • 1 liter water
  • 2-3 pandan leaves, tied into a knot
  • Honey or additional sweetener (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the winter melon into small cubes.
  2. In a large pot, add the diced winter melon and water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 30-45 minutes until the winter melon becomes soft and translucent.
  4. Add the rock sugar to the pot and stir until it dissolves completely.
  5. If using, add the pandan leaves for additional fragrance and let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Use a potato masher or a spoon to mash the winter melon pieces in the pot to release more flavor.
  7. Let the mixture cool slightly, then strain the tea through a fine sieve or cheesecloth to remove the solids, leaving a clear winter melon tea.
  8. Taste the tea and adjust the sweetness if needed by adding honey or more rock sugar while itâ€™s still warm.
  9. Chill the tea in the refrigerator for a few hours or serve over ice for a refreshing cold drink.
  10. Can store in refrigerator 3 days
