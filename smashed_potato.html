Garlic and Herb Butter Smashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg baby potatoes, washed
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 40g (1/2 cup) finely grated parmesan

Garlic and herb butter

  • 100g butter, chopped, at room temperature
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 lemon, rind finely grated
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh continental parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/180C fan forced.
  2. Line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  3. Place potatoes in a large saucepan and cover with cold water.
  4. Season with salt.
  5. Bring to the boil over high heat.
  6. Boil for 10 minutes or until just tender.
  7. Drain. Set aside in a colander to cool.
  8. For the garlic and herb butter, place the butter, garlic, lemon rind, parsley and 2 tbs chives in a bowl. Season. Stir to combine.
  9. Divide the potatoes among the prepared trays.
  10. Use the palm of your hand to squash slightly.
  11. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with parmesan. Season. Roast for 30 minutes or until golden.
  12. Top potatoes with butter mixture.
  13. Roast for a further 15-20 minutes or until golden and crisp.
  14. Sprinkle with remaining 1 tbs chives.

Allergens

This recipe may contain milk and lactose.
