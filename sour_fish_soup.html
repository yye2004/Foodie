Chinese Sour Fish Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 whole fish (about 1.5 pounds), cleaned and sliced
  • 2 cups pickled mustard greens, sliced
  • 1 large piece of ginger, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 tablespoon Sichuan peppercorns
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the fish slices under cold water and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, add the water, ginger, garlic, green onions, dried red chilies, and Sichuan peppercorns. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Add the pickled mustard greens to the pot and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Add the fish slices to the pot and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through.
  5. Season with soy sauce, rice vinegar, and salt to taste.
  6. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot.
