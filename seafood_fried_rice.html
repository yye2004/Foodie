Seafood Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 200g shrimp
  • 100g scallops
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add shrimp and scallops, and cook until done.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, and mix well.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Allergens

This dish contains seafood (shrimp, scallops) and soy.
