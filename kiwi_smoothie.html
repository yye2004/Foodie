Green Kiwi Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe kiwis, peeled and chopped
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the kiwis.
  2. Peel the banana and break it into chunks.
  3. Combine all ingredients in a blender: kiwis, banana, spinach, Greek yogurt, orange juice, honey (if using), and ice cubes.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Allergens

This recipe contains dairy (Greek yogurt).
