Colour-changing Drink

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup lime coola cordial
  • 1/3 cup raspberry cordial
  • 1/3 cup tropical cordial
  • 3 litres sparkling mineral water, chilled

Steps

  1. Divide lime coola, raspberry and tropical cordials evenly among four 12-hole (1 tablespoon-capacity) ice cube trays, keeping flavours separate, until each hole is 1/4 full. Carefully add enough sparkling mineral water to fill each hole (you'll need about 3 cups).
  2. Freeze overnight or until firm.
  3. Place 2 ice cubes of each colour into 8 serving glasses.
  4. Top up with remaining mineral water. Serve immediately.
