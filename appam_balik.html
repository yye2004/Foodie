Appam Balik

Ingredients

  • 150g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 250ml water
  • 100g roasted peanuts, crushed
  • 50g sweet corn
  • 2 tbsp sugar (for filling)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and sugar.
  2. Add egg and water to the dry ingredients, mix until smooth batter forms.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, grease lightly with butter.
  4. Pour a ladle of batter into the pan, spreading evenly to form a thin pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then sprinkle crushed peanuts, sweet corn, and sugar evenly.
  6. Fold the pancake in half and press gently to stick.
  7. Remove from pan when both sides are golden brown.
  8. Cut into wedges and serve warm.

Allergens

This dish contains peanuts, egg, and dairy (butter).
