Homemade Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, about 3-4 pounds, cut into pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 large piece of ginger, sliced
  • 6 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced daikon radish
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the chicken pieces under cold water and place them in a large pot.
  2. Add the water, ginger, green onions, and garlic to the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1 hour, skimming any foam that rises to the surface.
  4. Add the shiitake mushrooms, carrots, and daikon radish to the pot. Continue to simmer for another 30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with soy sauce and salt to taste.
  6. Remove the chicken pieces and shred the meat, discarding the bones.
  7. Return the shredded chicken to the soup and stir to combine.
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot.
