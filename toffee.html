Strawberry Toffee Desserts

Ingredients

  • 500g strawberries, hulled, quartered (You can use any other summer berries instead of strawberries â€“ or sliced peaches or nectarines or a mixture.)
  • 1/4 cup orange-flavoured liqueur (such as Cointreau or Grand Marnier
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar mixture
  • 350g Natural Strawberry yogurt
  • 3/4 cup caster sugar

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, liqueur and icing sugar in a bowl. Set aside for 10 minutes to allow flavours to develop.
  2. Divide strawberry mixture between glasses. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  3. Top with yoghurt.
  4. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, combine caster sugar and 3/4 cup warm water in a saucepan over low heat. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Increase heat to high. Bring to the boil.
  6. Boil, without stirring, for 10 minutes or until mixture is golden brown.
  7. Remove from heat. Set aside for 2 minutes or until bubbles subside.
  8. Spoon sugar mixture over yoghurt. Serve.

Allergens

This recipe may contain milk, lactose and alcohol.
