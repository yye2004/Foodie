Rosemary, Garlic and Chilli Popcorn

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, lightly bashed
  • 1 tsp chipotle or other chilli flakes
  • 1/2 small bunch of rosemary, finely chopped
  • 150g popcorn kernels

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over a medium heat.
  2. Fry the garlic, chilli and rosemary for 2-3 mins.
  3. Remove from the heat, set aside and leave the oil to infuse for 30 mins.
  4. Add the popcorn kernels and cover the pot with a lid.
  5. Once the popcorn starts popping, shake the pot occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  6. When the popping slows down, remove the pot from heat.
  7. Scoop the garlic out of the infused oil and discard.
  8. Toss the popcorn with the oil, then season and serve straightaway.
