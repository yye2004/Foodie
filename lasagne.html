Lemon Dessert Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups milk
  • 16 Dutch speculaas cookies
  • 1kg fresh ricotta
  • 1 1/2 tbsp finely grated lemon rind
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2/3 cup caster sugar
  • 100g packet vanilla flavoured dessert mix
  • 1/2 cup lemon butter, softened
  • 2 tbsp boiling water
  • 1 tbsp gelatine powder
  • 300ml thickened cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Lemon slices, to decorate
  • Small fresh mint leaves, to decorate

Steps

  1. Remove milk from fridge. Stand for 30 minutes to allow to come to room temperature.
  2. Arrange biscuits, in a single layer, over the base of a 4cm-deep, 20cm x 28cm (base) baking dish, trimming biscuits to fit if necessary.
  3. Place ricotta, lemon rind, lemon juice and 1/2 cup sugar in a food processor. Process until smooth and combined.
  4. Evenly spread ricotta mixture over biscuits.
  5. Refrigerate until required.
  6. Make vanilla dessert, using milk and following packet directions. Whisk in lemon butter.
  7. Make vanilla dessert, using milk and following packet directions. Whisk in lemon butter.
  8. Sprinkle over gelatine.
  9. Stir until gelatine dissolves.
  10. Whisk gelatine into lemon mixture.
  11. Refrigerate for 15 minutes or until mixture thickens slightly.
  12. Gently pour lemon mixture over ricotta mixture.
  13. Refrigerate for 4 hours or until set.
  14. Using an electric mixer, beat cream, vanilla and remaining sugar until just-firm peaks form.
  15. Spread cream over lemon layer.
  16. Decorate with lemon slices and mint leaves. Serve.

Allergens

Recipe may contain gluten, wheat, egg, sulphites and milk.
